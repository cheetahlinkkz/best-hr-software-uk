Top HR Software Solutions for UK Businesses

Discover the best HR software UK that fits your business needs, whether you're a small, medium, or growing enterprise. These tools can help automate HR processes, manage compliance, and improve the overall employee experience.

BreatheHR

BreatheHR is a user-friendly HR software ideal for small to medium-sized businesses, offering tools for absence management, performance tracking, and document storage.

  • Absence management and holiday tracking
  • Performance reviews and goal tracking
  • Employee record management
  • Secure document storage
  • Customizable reporting

Zoho People

Zoho People provides comprehensive HR features including attendance tracking, recruitment, and performance management. It's a scalable option for businesses of all sizes.

  • Employee self-service portal
  • Attendance and time tracking
  • Leave management and scheduling
  • Performance appraisal management
  • Customizable workflows

Sage HR

Sage HR offers a powerful HR platform that includes performance management, leave tracking, and employee record management. It's a great choice for growing businesses.

  • Employee record management
  • Time tracking and leave management
  • Performance management and appraisals
  • Customizable reporting
  • Integration with other Sage software

Cezar HR

Cezar HR is a flexible HR software solution designed for small to medium-sized businesses, offering customizable tools for managing employee leave, training, and performance.

  • Holiday and absence management
  • Employee training and development tracking
  • Performance management
  • HR reporting tools for compliance
  • Customizable HR workflows

Personio

Personio is an all-in-one HR software solution ideal for mid-sized companies, covering employee data management, payroll integration, recruiting, and performance management.

  • Employee data management
  • Recruiting and onboarding
  • Time tracking and leave management
  • Payroll integration
  • Performance management and feedback tools
